Here's a time line of Nikola Tesla:

  • 1856 - Tesla was born in Croatia
  • 1870's - Tesla studies in Germany & Austria
  • 1884 - Tesla travels to the United States and begins working for Thomas Edison
  • 1885 - Tesla founded "Tesla Electric Light Company" though short lived
  • 1887 - Tesla starts his new company "Tesla Electric Company" with AC system technologies
  • 1888 - George Westinghouse invests in Teslas Company
  • 1893 - Tesla wins contract to light the Columbian Exposition over Edison
  • 1895 - Tesla design first hydroelectric plant in Niagara Falls
  • 1900 - Tesla begins work on his wireless communication system
  • 1901 - Tesla builds his transmission tower on Long Island, New York
  • 1906 - Transmission tower staff was laid off due to competition funded by Edison
  • 1915 - Tower goes into foreclosure
  • 1918 - Tesla declares bankruptcy
  • 1943 - Tesla dies from coronary thrombosis in NYC
